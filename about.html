Quality Policy

  • At Centillion Chemicals, quality is an integral part of the core business values that ensures promising actions about delivering high caliber products.
  • An ISO 9001-2015 certified company, Centillion Chemicals Pvt Ltd erects on the plinth of quality. Complying with our core value i.e. ‘quality’, we ensure distinctive manufacturing and maintenance of goods and services respectively.
  • Adhering to our Quality Management System, each product and activity is processed under strict controls and checks in order to have a quality and a reliable outcome.
  • Regular Workshops with the Fortis Team for enhancing attributes and upgrading quality index.
  • Raw Material Purchase from Approved Suppliers only, assuring authenticity from the initial to the end stage.
  • Timely Review and Surprise Scrutiny, the manufacturing plant, the equipments and the processes are punctually reviewed and scrutinized in order to ensure non-negotiable quality.
  • Audits and Formal Assessments, compliance against the ISO 9001: 2015 standard requires the conduct of regular structured internal audits of our processes and formal review meetings to assess our business performance.
  • At Centillion Chemicals, we believe and bring into work, the effortless exercise of resorting to such practices that nucleus excellence. We believe in distinctive culture and in that order, every team member is encouraged to participate in the search for continual improvement in quality.

Environment Management

  • As per the guideline of SPCB due care has been taken to control the emissions by installing of Multi stage Cyclone Separators in Exhaust line of TFH, Bag Filters and dust extraction Systems in Fine Grinding Unit.
  • Sprinkling of water time to for suppression of fugitive dust emissions inside the Plant due to vehicle movement.
  • Roof Extractors are installed to keep the plant well ventilated.
  • We have planted number of saplings in & around the plant to improve green belt & made concrete approach road to the plant with road side gardening to reduce dust emission as well as improving the aesthetics of the place.
